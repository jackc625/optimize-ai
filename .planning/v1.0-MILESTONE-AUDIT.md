## Integration Check

### Cross-Phase Wiring: 14/15 requirement paths wired

All 15 requirements have verified import-to-usage chains except one partial connection:

**SAFE-04 partial wiring:** `dashboard/page.tsx` `handleLogout` (line 63-65) calls `supabase.auth.signOut()` then `router.push('/auth/login')` without clearing the `sb-authed` cookie. The parallel handler in `dashboard/layout.tsx` correctly clears the cookie. The `useUser` `onAuthStateChange` listener eventually clears it, but there is a brief window where middleware admits a signed-out user.

**Fix:** Add `document.cookie = "sb-authed=; path=/; expires=Thu, 01 Jan 1970 00:00:00 GMT";` before `router.push` in `dashboard/page.tsx` line 64.
